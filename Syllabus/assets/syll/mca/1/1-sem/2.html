PROBABILITY AND STATISTICS

UNIT-1

Data Validation and information abstraction: Methods of collecting data efficiently, gathering information from data charting.

UNIT-2

Probability : Laws of Probability Probability distributions, Discrete, equiprobable binomial, Poisson.

UNIT-3

Continuous distributions : Rectangular, normal, gamma and beta.

UNIT-4

Statistical methods: Frequency distributions, mathematical expecta­tion, moment, skewness and kurtosis.

UNIT-5

Correlation and regression, Introduction to tests of significance, u, t, x tests.
