Cycle - I

1. Direct tension test on metal rods

4. Compression test

5. Impact test

Cycle – II

1. Test on a helical spring to determine the rigidity modulus.

2. Torsion test to determine the rigidity modulus of a shaft.

3. Deflection test on a cantilever beam to determine the Young’s modulus

4. Deflection test on a simple beam to determine the Young’s modulus

5. Deflection test on a fixed beam to determine the Young’s modulus

6. Fatigue test.
