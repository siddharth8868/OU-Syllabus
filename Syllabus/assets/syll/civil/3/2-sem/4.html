UNIT-I

Combined Footing and Retaining walls: Limit state design & detailing of combined rectangular and trapezoidal footings and retaining walls – cantilever and counter fort types.

UNIT-II

Water Tanks: Elastic Design & Detailing for RCC circular land rectangular ground level and over-head tanks-Design of staging. Design of Intze tanks.

UNIT-III

Bridges: IRC loadings; Elastic design and detailing of (i) RC bridge deck slab using effective width methods and Pigeaud’s method, (ii) Slab Bridges, and (iii) T-beam bridges.
