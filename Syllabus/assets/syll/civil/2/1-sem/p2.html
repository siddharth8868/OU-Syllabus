List of Experiments

1. Use of chain and tape and other accessories required for linear measurements.

2. Traversing by using chain and tape.

3. Application of traversing to locate a building and field objects.

4. Traversing by using compass.

5. Distance between two inaccessible points by compass survey.

6. Compass traversing and adjustment of closed traverse by Bowditch's method.

7. Plane table survey; radiation and intersection methods.

8. Two-point problem.

9. Three-point problem using trial and error method.

10. Flying levels using Dumpy Level.

11. Tilting level; L.S. and C.S.

12. Demonstration of minor surveying instruments.
