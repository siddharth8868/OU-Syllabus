UNIT-I

Beams curved in plan : Introduction – Design Principles – Structural Design of beams curved in plan of circular and rectangular types.

Deep Beams : Introduction – flexural and shear stresses in deep beams.

– I.S. Code provisions – design of Deep beams.

UNIT-II

Building Frames : Substitute frame method of analysis for building frames-design of rectangular portal frames for vertical loading including hinges at the base- Detailing of frames.

Flat slabs : Introduction, Components- I.S. Code Provisions – Design methods, Design for flexure and shear – Openings in Flat slabs

UNIT-III

Pile foundations – Structural design of piles and pile caps

Raft Foundations : Definitions, Types – Structural analysis and design of Raft foundation for buildings with column grids up to three by two
