UNIT-I

Plate Girder : Limit State Design of riveted and welded plate girder for static loads - including flange curtailment, connections, Intermediate and bearing stiffeners, Web and flange splice.

UNIT-II

Crane and Gantry girders : Basic Principles, Codal Provisions, Limit state design of single bay portal with and without crane including detailing. Bearings: Types – Rocker and Roller – Elastic Design of bearings for bridges.

UNIT-III

Bridges : Deck and through type bridges – Economical span – Indian standard Railway broad gauge train loadings – permissible stresses – Detailed design and drawing of plate girder and truss bridges using elastic design.
