CS 333

MINI PROJECT

Instruction 3 Periods per week

Sessional 25 Marks

The students are required to carry out mini projects in any of the areas such as Data Structures, Microprocessors and Interfacing, Database Management Systems, Operating Systems, Design and Analysis of Algorithms, and Software Engineering.

Students are required to submit a report on the mini project at the end of the semester.
